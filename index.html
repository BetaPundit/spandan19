  • About Us

    SVVV's Spandan is cultural fest power packed with fun and entertainment which will leave the attendees in euphoria. For this we are equipped with buzzing events like Pronites, Silent Disco, Nirvana Rock Concert, Ambriti - The Fashion Show, Swaranjali - The Singing Face-off and Taal - The Dance Battle. All this coupled up with the Food Fiesta and our in-house shenanigans.

    Other fun events involve treasure hunt, sketching, face painting, fireless cooking, etc. to engage and promote interaction among the audience and enrich the culture of the overall fest.
